SOCIALS

GO GREEN

REGISTER

  • ENTRY FEE: Rs.300/- (8-20 Participants)
    Go green is a group activity in which, within a given time, groups must perform The team social activities.
  • RULES:
    • Select the area of your choice you want to develop and improve.
    • Area should be in Pune.
    • Team should consist of maximum 20 members & minimum 8 members.
    • If same area is preferred by many teams then it will be allotted on first come first serve basis.
    • Activity must be completed in time.
    • Requirement sheet has to be submitted before the event.
    • Basic things from the Requirements sheet will be provided (brooms, garbage bags, etc.) by the college.
    • If participants are NSS members, then their hours will be counted.
    • Participants are requested to be present at the venue 20 mins before the reporting time.
    • It is mandatory for every participant to carry their respective receipts without which entry will not be permitted.
    • Every participant should have his/her own valid I-Card.
    • Decision of the judges and organizing team will be final and binding.

REGISTER

  • RULES:

SANKRAMAN

REGISTER

  • Entry fee: Rs. 50/- (1 participant)
    A short film will be shown, on which participants have to write an abstract. GD will be conducted on another short film showed.

  • RULES:
    • There are two rounds in the event.
    • Firstly, a movie will be shown to the participants related to any particular topic & then participants have to write an abstract inferring the movie. (Maximum 9 mins.)
    • The participants are then shortlisted for the second round.
    • In the second round, a movie will be shown on which the participants are expected to speak for maximum 3.5min.
    • The participants will be judged by external faculty pursuing knowledge of the related topics.
    • Judgment is done completely on the basis of presentation skills and quick thinking about the related topic.
    • Participants are requested to be present at the venue 20 mins before the reporting time.
    • It is mandatory for every participant to carry their respective receipts without which entry will not be permitted.
    • Every participant should have his/her own valid I-Card.
    • Decision of the judges and organizing team will be final and binding.

SOCIO-MASHUP

REGISTER

  • Entry fee: Rs. 50/- (1 Participant)
    A topic will be given to the participants on which they have to make, write and express in the poetic form.

  • RULES:
    • An entry shall consist of max 1 person.
    • The participants shall not be allowed to use mobile, electronic instruments or any kind of sheet.
    • Participants are requested to be present at the venue 20 mins before the reporting time.
    • It is mandatory for every participant to carry their respective receipts without which entry will not be permitted.
    • Every participant should have his/her own valid I-Card.
    • Decision of the judges and organizing team will be final and binding.
    • First round: Participants have to make a poetry based on the topics given to them, within the time allotted. (i.e. 30 mins)
    • Second round: Participants will be given an introductory part of the poem and will have to complete the poem with their own words within the allotted time.
    • Participants will be allotted 1.30 min to present their poem in front of the judges.
